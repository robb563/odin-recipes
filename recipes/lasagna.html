How to make lasagna

Making lasanga can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • cheeses
  • and eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe bellow.
    • Meat sauce
    • Noodles
    • Ricotta mixture
    • Mozzerella slices
    • Meat sauce
    • parmesan cheese
    • Repeat the layers
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
